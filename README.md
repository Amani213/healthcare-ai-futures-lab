# 🏥 Healthcare AI Futures Lab

> *Learn the future before it arrives.*

A free, open-source clinical AI governance simulation for NHS leaders, clinicians, digital professionals, and regulators. Built as an interactive HTML tool — no installation, no login, no data collected.

**[▶ Play the demo](https://healthcare-ai-futures-lab.vercel.app/)** · [Report an issue](../../issues) · [Contribute](#contributing)

-----

## What is this?

Healthcare AI Futures Lab is a decision simulation that places you inside real NHS AI governance dilemmas. Every choice has second and third-order consequences. There are no perfect answers.

The current release is a **proof of concept** featuring the first playable scenario:

> **Level 2 — Clinical Safety Officer: The Sepsis Signal Problem**
> 
> An AI sepsis detector is generating alarm fatigue across your Trust. A patient has deteriorated after nurses dismissed an alert they believed was another false positive. You have 4 decision rounds and 48 hours to act.

The simulation tracks four interconnected metrics — Patient Safety, Staff Trust, Innovation, and Financial Sustainability — and generates a full debrief tied to NHS clinical AI governance frameworks.

-----

## Why does this exist?

The NHS is deploying AI into clinical workflows faster than governance frameworks, workforce capability, and patient safety infrastructure can keep pace.

This tool exists to close that gap through experiential learning — putting decision-makers inside the complexity before they face it in practice.

It is designed for:

- 🩺 **Clinicians** encountering AI-assisted decision support for the first time
- 🛡️ **Clinical Safety Officers** navigating DCB0129/0160 obligations
- 💻 **CNIOs and digital leaders** managing workforce adoption
- 🏛️ **NHS executives and board members** making AI investment decisions
- 🎓 **Educators** in healthcare, nursing, medicine, and health informatics
- ⚖️ **Regulators and policy professionals** stress-testing governance frameworks

## Governance Frameworks Referenced

The simulation is grounded in real NHS and UK regulatory standards:

- **DCB0129** — Clinical Risk Management for Health IT Systems (manufacturer)
- **DCB0160** — Clinical Risk Management for Health IT Systems (deploying organisation)
- **MHRA AI as a Medical Device** guidance
- **LFPSE** — Learn from Patient Safety Events
- **CQC** regulatory expectations for digital safety
- **UK GDPR Article 22** — automated decision-making
- **NHS AI governance frameworks** (NHSX, NHS England)

## How to Use

### Play it now

Open `healthcare-ai-futures-lab.html` directly in any modern browser. No server required.

### Host on GitHub Pages

1. Fork this repository
1. Go to **Settings → Pages**
1. Set source to `main` branch, root folder
1. Your simulation will be live at `https://yourusername.github.io/healthcare-ai-futures-lab`

### Use in a training programme

The simulation runs entirely in-browser with no external dependencies. You can:

- Share the link before a workshop for pre-reading/play
- Use it live in group sessions with discussion after each round
- Assign it as standalone e-learning
- Host it on your organisation’s intranet by copying the single HTML file

A **facilitator guide** (with suggested debrief questions per round) is in development.

## Design Principles

1. **Decision-rich environments** — every choice has second and third-order consequences
1. **No perfect answers** — the simulation reflects real governance dilemmas, not training manuals
1. **Hidden system dynamics** — the player never sees the full formula; delayed consequences emerge over time
1. **Pedagogical grounding** — debrief tied to named frameworks, not generic reflection prompts
1. **NHS-authentic** — scenarios, roles, regulatory context, and stakeholder tensions drawn from real practice
